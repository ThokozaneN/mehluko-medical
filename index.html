Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your ID, medical aid card, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results from previous providers.

Do you offer emergency services?

We provide urgent care during business hours for non-life-threatening conditions. For serious emergencies, please visit your nearest hospital emergency department.

How do I get my test results?

Test results are typically available within 3-5 business days. We will contact you with results that require immediate attention. You can also access your results through our patient portal.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours notice for appointment cancellations. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.

Stay Active
